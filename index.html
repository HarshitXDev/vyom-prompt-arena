Overview

Roast My Prompt Battle is an interactive competition where participants compete to create the most creative, funny, or impressive AI-generated output using prompts. Organized by GfG Campus Body – RIU as part of VYOM 2026 Annual Fest, this event introduces students to prompt engineering — one of the most important skills in modern AI interaction.

Participants will write prompts and use AI tools such as ChatGPT, DALL·E, or Midjourney to generate results. The goal is to show that the quality of AI output depends on the creativity and clarity of the prompt. Beginner-friendly and open to all students, even those with no coding experience.

Objectives:

  • Introduce students to AI tools and prompt engineering
  • Encourage creativity and critical thinking
  • Create an interactive and entertaining tech fest activity
  • Promote awareness about AI technologies

Rules
  • No personal attacks, hate speech, or offensive language of any kind
  • Humor must focus on technology and developer culture only
  • Participants must keep their roast strictly within the time limit
  • Judges' or students' poll decision will be final and binding
  • Individual participation only — solo registration required
  • AI tools must be used as specified per round (ChatGPT, DALL·E, or Midjourney)
Guidelines

This event is designed to be fun, beginner-friendly, and open to all students — even those with no prior coding or AI experience. Participants are encouraged to be creative, explore AI tools freely, and engage with the audience confidently.

All prompts and generated outputs will be displayed publicly on the projector screen. Participants should ensure their content is appropriate for a college audience and strictly within the theme of technology and developer culture. Any participant found violating the spirit of the event may be disqualified at the judges' discretion.

Judging Criteria
01
Creativity & Prompt Design
How unique and clever the participant's prompt is. Judges look at how creatively the prompt is structured to guide the AI toward an interesting or unexpected result.
02
Humor & Audience Engagement
How entertaining the prompt and its output are for the audience. Participants who make the crowd laugh, react, or stay engaged will score higher.
03
Technical Relevance
How well the prompt demonstrates understanding of AI prompting techniques — clear instructions, context, constraints, and structured prompts.
04
Confidence & Delivery
How confidently the participant presents their prompt, explains their idea, and interacts with the audience during the battle.
05
Quality of AI Output
How effective the prompt is in generating a meaningful, impressive, or funny AI response.
